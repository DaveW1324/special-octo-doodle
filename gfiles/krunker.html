3D First Person Shooter Gun HTML5 Minecraft Multiplayer Pixel Weapon

Krunker.io is a fast-paced pixelated first-person shooter. In this game, drop into a pixelated world and fight against other players from around the world. Krunker.io has so much to offer and is a heap of fun. This game can be played casually to pass the time, or seriously in competition against other die-hards.
SHOW MORE

Before fighting, choose from one of 11 different classes including detective, rocketeer, agent, runner, Bowman, and hunter. Each class has a different style and outfit. Moreover, each class has a different primary and secondary weapon. The triggerman, for example, carries an assault rifle and pistol.

Aside from the many classes, this game also has a range of awesome weapons. Choose from deadly weapons such as an assault rifle, sniper rifle, shotgun, revolver or even akimbo uzis. Each weapon has different advantages - the sniper, for example, has a scope and is great for long-range fights.

As far as shooter games go, this one is so much fun. The actual first-person gameplay is hectic and fast-paced. The characters move quickly and players must be on their guard to avoid being hit. Furthermore, players must also have a quick aim and a steady hand.

Another feature that makes this game stand out is the awesome selection of maps. Users can submit map creations of their own - there are literally hundreds of different maps to choose from. The creativity of the map designs is superb - play on anything from rocky lava landscapes to ancient Aztec pyramids.

Finally, players can track their scores in the social section. Simply click on social and head to the Krunker.io website - this shows leaderboards and other stats. See if you can gain a spot on the leaderboard today with your pixelated fighting skills.

Release Date

Krunker was released in June 2018 and has been continuously updated since then.

Features

  • More than 5 characters to play with
  • You can spray paint
  • Fast-paced gameplay
  • Smooth controls
  • Play Krunker in fullscreen by pressing the fullscreen icon. You can find the fullscreen icon in the bottom right corner of the game.
  • Spectate mode if you want to just watch the gameplay.

Platform

Krunker is a web browser game. Currently, it is not available on mobile devices such as iOS and Android devices.

Developer

Krunker is made by Sidney de Vries, who has previously made other .io games such as MooMoo.

Tips & Tricks

  • Try out different guns, choose one depending on the circumstances.
  • Seek shelter while reloading.
  • If you jump while shooting someone you can earn extra points!
  • Practice a lot! It's a great game but it requires skills you only gain if you train.
  • Check out the Krunker Twitch channel for gameplay videos and streaming.
  • You can host your own Krunker.io matches by click Host Game, selecting the maps, classes, and modes that you want, and then sharing URL or code with your friends.
  • You can join a specific Krunker game by clicking join and entering the game's URL or code.
  • The game has its own merchandise, which you can find here! Only for the true fans...

Controls

How to play Krunker.io
  • Use the WASD keys to move
  • Left click to shoot
  • R to reload
  • C to aim / zoom
  • Space bar to jump
  • Shift to crouch
  • F to spray paint
